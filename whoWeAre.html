Our Story

CareerCatalyst Africa was inspired by Oluwaseun Atanda's personal experience of struggles finding professional job to continues his career after relocating to the United kingdom with his spouse . Established in 2024, our organization is driven by a passion for creating positive change in the lives of partners and spouses of healthcare professionals.
